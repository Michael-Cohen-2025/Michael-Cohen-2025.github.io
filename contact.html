About / Contact Me



I’m a mechanical engineering student at Florida Atlantic University (expected graduation Dec 2026) who genuinely enjoys the process of taking an idea from a rough concept to a clean, buildable design. I’m most at home working through the CAD-to-validation workflow—modeling in SolidWorks/NX, checking decisions with FEA tools like ANSYS, and keeping an eye on the practical details that make something manufacturable and easy to assemble.

Through internships, campus roles, and project work, I’ve leaned into hands-on engineering that blends technical rigor with real constraints. I’ve supported manufacturing and build-prep efforts where traceability, clear work instructions, and clean data matter, and I’ve also worked on projects spanning thermal/fluid analysis, embedded/mechatronics, and data-driven process improvement. This portfolio highlights a few of those projects—focused on solving real problems, iterating quickly, and communicating results clearly.
